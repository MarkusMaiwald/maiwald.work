Part 2: Maiwald Enterprises BV: Architecting the "Company Services" Section
This section provides the complete content and structure for the "Company Services" page of the Maiwald Enterprises BV website. The content is designed to establish the firm as a high-end, strategic technology partner, building trust and clearly articulating its value proposition to attract high-value clients. The tone is professional, confident, and client-benefit-oriented.   

2.1. The Value Proposition: Strategic Leadership for Complex Solutions
The core message must immediately differentiate the company from commoditized development agencies. The emphasis is on strategic architecture, oversight, and the delivery of bespoke solutions. The user's non-developer role is framed as a key strength: he provides the high-level vision and project governance essential for success.

Proposed Headline:

Architecting Your Technological Advantage

Proposed Introductory Paragraph:

Maiwald Enterprises BV is not a software development agency; we are a strategic technology partner for organizations with ambitious goals. We specialize in architecting and delivering bespoke solutions for your most complex challenges in blockchain, cloud, and custom systems. Led by technology architect [User's Name], we provide the crucial strategic oversight and access to elite development talent required to transform visionary ideas into robust, scalable, and market-defining realities.

The remote nature of the team must be presented as a strategic advantage—access to a global pool of elite talent—while the user's physical presence provides the high-touch strategic partnership clients value.

Our Model: Globally Sourced Expertise, Locally Delivered Strategy

Since individual team members are not listed, it is crucial to build trust by describing the quality, vetting process, and nature of the professional network. This communicates exclusivity and reliability.   

Proposed Section Title:

The Maiwald Enterprises Network: A Team Built on Trust

Proposed Content:

We believe that exceptional outcomes are the product of exceptional teams. The Maiwald Enterprises BV network is not an open marketplace of anonymous freelancers. Conclusions and Recommendations
The provided analysis and drafted content offer a comprehensive solution for developing the "About Me" and "Company Services" sections of the user's portfolio website. The core strategic recommendations are as follows:

Fuse the Personal and Professional: The most powerful brand narrative does not separate the user's "hobbies" from his professional work. Instead, it frames the personal projects (OS development, TTRPG) as the R&D engine that fuels his professional expertise and strategic insight. This transforms him from a manager of developers into a visionary architect who understands technology at a uniquely deep level.

Leverage the TTRPG as a Case Study: The TTRPG project is the single most valuable asset for communication. It serves as a compelling, understandable, and innovative vehicle for explaining complex and highly valuable concepts like Self-Sovereign Identity and Decentralized Identifiers. It demonstrates not just technical capability but also the creative and strategic vision to find novel applications for emerging technologies.

Position Maiwald Enterprises BV as a Strategic Partner: The company's value proposition should be centered on high-level architecture, strategic oversight, and access to an elite, curated network of specialists. The user's role as a non-developer is a strength, allowing him to maintain the strategic vision necessary for complex project success. The "Global Talent, Local Strategy" model is a key differentiator that should be highlighted.

Prioritize Clarity and Client Value: All service descriptions should be framed in terms of the strategic value and tangible benefits they deliver to a client's business. The use of structured formats, such as the proposed Service Offering & Value Matrix, is recommended to enhance clarity and professionalism.

By implementing this content strategy, the user can create a portfolio website that not only showcases his skills and services but also builds a powerful, authentic, and differentiated personal and corporate brand.
